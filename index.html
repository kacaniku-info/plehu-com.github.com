    Welcome to the Kosovo Fertilizer Project.

    This is the presentation site for Adler Holding LLC's "Kosovo Fertilizer Project". To join the team of investors scroll to the bottom of this page and directly buy shares in the KFP!

    The KFP will be the first-ever Kosovar facility that can produce premium organic fertilizer. Our product is sterile and safe, of premium quality, in line with european standards, and with nutritional values comparable to chemical fertilizer.

    The process will be conducted by a Made in UK, "Rolls Royce of its category" production line that sterilizes, dries and pelletizes organic fertilizer at premium quality.

    This project aims to directly invest in the town of Gjakova, and create so new employment, facilitate integration and rehabilitate the polluted soil that makes it hard for farmers to make a living.
