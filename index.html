    Welcome to the Kosovo Fertilizer Project.

    This is the presentation site for Adler Holding LLC's "Kosovo Fertilizer Project" that aims to establish the first-ever kosovar organic fertilizer production facility that can produce a uniform, sterile, pelletized and safe organic fertilizer of european standards. The process will be conducted by a Made in UK, "Rolls Royce of its category" production line that sterilizes, dries and pelletizes organic fertilizer at premium quality.

    This project aims to directly invest in the impoverished former industrial town of Mitrovica, and create so new employment, facilitate integration and rehabilitate the polluted soil that makes it hard for farmers to make a living.
